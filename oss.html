  • Location : NIT Rourkela

This logo was designed for a newly formed club in NIT-R named "Odia Sahitya Samaj". The logo has the illustration of Konark Chakra which is a symbol of Odia Culture. Moreover feather was used to symbolize that the language has deep existence in the history. The splitting ink gives motion to the logo. Odia typeface was used for the text part of the logo.
