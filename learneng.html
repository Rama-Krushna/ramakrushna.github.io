Design Process

Coming to the project, LearnEng is a mobile app design of a month-long solo project. I conducted the end-to-end research and design process, from conducting interviews to validating design decisions by testing with target users. My design evolved along with several rounds of iteration at different stages.



Research

I began the project by conducting a user survey to collect various information from the users. It was followed by conducting user interview, empathy mapping, and competitive analysis to gain knowledge to further carry on with the process.


User Survey

I conducted the user survey to gather data on some important details like - the kind of courses people prefer, key learning strategies to learn a new language, etc.
